When you choose Happy Egg

We're Happy Egg

  • March 21, 2023
  • Love Human
  • 321 Likes
  • 21 Comments

We're a Free Range egg company that believes in making intentional choices, because we've seen the lasting impact a single choice can make. Throughout the entirety of our business, we make deliberate decisions for the betterment of our hens and our eggs so that you can make the easy choice when shopping for eggs: to Choose Happy.

The heart of everything we do begins on our farms, which is why we choose to partner with small family farmers to raise our hens. Nestled throughout what we like to call the Heartland of Happy, our Growers provide our hens with the Happy standard of care as they roam on 8+ acres daily, enjoy premium feed, and graze on grass and bugs.

This leads to eggs with delicious golden yolks, laid by hens who are thriving. In turn, a farmer is able to provide security and freedom for their own family. That's a win-win-win.

Inside every Happy Egg

You'll find a thick shell, plump orange yolks, and creamy whites. We choose to feed our hens a premium, nutritious diet that, when coupled with their Happy living on 8+ acres, produces an egg that's different in both appearance and taste. Plus, our Happy hens are worth the pampering, don't you think?
